Task 20-2

You are participating in a competition. All participant tickets have numbers in the range [1016; 1937]. Each participant can choose any free ticket with a number from this range.
In secret, you were told that the winner is the ticket whose number satisfies the following conditions

  1. divisible by 3 and 7
  2. not divisible by 5 and 2
Of these, the ticket with the highest number is the winning one.

To win, you need to find out the ticket number and select it. Since a manual search will be long and irrational, then write a program that will find it for you.
What is the winning ticket number?
