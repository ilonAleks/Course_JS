Task 9-6

The last thing Alexei wants to see on his website is a coffee shop system of assessments. The owner needs to know customer opinions to correct defects in their work.

First you need to create an empty arrayclientsEstimations, in which customer ratings will be stored coffee shops. Also create a function askClientToGiveEstimation, which should display the message “How do you evaluate our Coffee shop from 1 to 10? " through prompt. The final result that the user will introduce should be a type number data. If the number from 1 to 10 was introduced, then add this assessment to the array clientsEstimations, otherwise no action perform.

To add assessments, call the function askClientToGiveEstimation Five times. Recommended to do this through the cycle for.

After the estimates are added, you need to calculate positive and negative assessments. Positive assessment is the number more than 5, negative - the number less in 5 or equal to 5. Derived through alert message “All positive assessments: goodEstimations; All negative assessments: notGoodEstimations”, where goodEstimations - the number of positive assessments, and notGoodEstimations - the number of negative assessments.

Conditions:

  1. The solution must be used in the solution filter.
