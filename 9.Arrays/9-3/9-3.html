Task 9-3

The array of numbers is given to you.

numbers = [10, 4, 100, -5, 54, 2];

It is necessary to calculate the amount of cubes of all numbers in the array. For example, for an array [1, 5, 9] should be the following result:
1^3 + 5^3 + 9^3 = 1 + 125 + 729 = 855

Solve this problem in 4 ways:

  1. Through the cycle for
  2. Through the cycle for of
  3. Through the method forEach
  4. Through the method reduce
For this array numbers The amount of cubes should be equal 1158411.
