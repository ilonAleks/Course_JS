Task 11-2

Birthday is a significant holiday for every person. And when is your birthday? I'm sure you remember this date. And how many days are left before your birth? But this is more complicated.

Now your task is to create a function getDaysBeforeBirthday, which will be return the quantity days until the next birthday. This function should take 1 parameter nextBirthdayDate, which the is the object of date indicates the date of your closest birthday. A year should indicate the closest to the current date, not year of your birth.

It is also recommended create an auxiliary function convertMsToDays, which translates milliseconds into days. For cutting the final number use Math.round.
