Imagine that we are developing a site for a restaurant in which the discount program operates. The restaurant calculates the amount of payment for each client encourages the most frequent visitors.

At the moment, the restaurant has a client named Igor, who for all the time spent 110$ in this institution. Save this data in variables clientName and clientSpentForAllTime.

  1. If the client spent from 100$ to 300$, then a 10%discount;
  2. If the client spent from 300$ to 500$, then the discount is 20%;
  3. If the client spent from 500$, then the discount is 30%.
A client named Igor decided to visit a restaurant again. As we see, he is supposed to be a 10% discount, since he spent 110$. Now Igor is purchased at 25$ and he needs to provide a discount. Save value 25 in the variable clientSpentToday.

First, implement the logic of discounts. Use if else and logical operators.The final discount save in a variable discount. Bring in a modal window through alert text “You are given a discount in discount%!” (discount replace the percentage of discounts).

After that, it is necessary to set a client named Igor for payment (given the discount). To begin with, update variable clientSpentForAllTime. Then bring in a modal window through alert message “Thank you, clientName! You will pay clientSpentToday$. For all the time in our restaurant you spent clientSpentForAllTime$.” (replace clientName, clientSpentToday and clientSpentForAllTime to the corresponding values of the variables)
